Project Overview

The Lakshana Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, product line contribution, profitability, and payment method trends across different regions of India. The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
Identify top-performing product lines.
Compare profitability across payment methods.
Enable regional analysis through interactive filters.
Provide actionable business insights for strategic planning.

🔍 Key Business Insights

Sold 87K units, indicating strong market demand.
Skincare is the highest revenue-generating product line with 66M sales.
Education and Food & Beverages contribute significantly with 65M sales each.
Home Décor generated 63M sales, showing consistent customer demand.
Technology contributed 61M sales and presents growth opportunities.
Profitability remains stable across all payment methods.
Debit Card, Credit Card, and Net Banking provide similar profit performance.
UPI shows slightly lower average profit compared to other payment methods.
Revenue distribution across multiple product categories reduces business risk.
